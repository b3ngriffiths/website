### [Saving Sessions](https://octopus.energy/saving-sessions/): 30.07 kWh saved

During 8 specified savings session during peak times, I ensured no grid import and exported to the grid from home battery. The National Grid paid £75.85 for this 'balancing the grid' support.

Across all Octopus Energy customers we saved 711,821 kWh, equivalent to boiling 24 million cups of tea and saved as much CO2 as 99,826 trees absorb in a year.
